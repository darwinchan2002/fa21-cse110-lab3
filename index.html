Idea of the App

  • It has been a headache for people who uses ND filters for taking long exposure photos. It is very time-consuming to caluculate the new shutter speed after using multiple stops of ND filters.

  • Therefore, we have decided to create an app for them!
    It will allow the user to first choose the ND filters / number of ND stops that they are using, and the original shutter speed.

    Then, the app will quickly calculate for the user how much time is needed for the shot they want. Long exposure photos become a piece of cake woohoo!
